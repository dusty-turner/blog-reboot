I'm honored to have served 18 years in the Army, currently as a LTC. My career began as an Engineer Officer and now I am an Operations Research Systems Analyst. I am the author of the [ggvfields package](https://github.com/dusty-turner/ggvfields) alongside [David Kahle](https://www.kahle.io/) and [Rodney Sturdivant](https://statistics.artsandsciences.baylor.edu/person/dr-rodney-x-sturdivant). I'm also grateful for the support of my wife, [Jill](https://dusty-turner.github.io/JillTurner/), and our two children—a 12-year-old son and a 10-year-old daughter.

I hold a PhD in Statistical Science from Baylor University (2025), an M.S. in Integrated Systems Engineering and a Graduate Minor in Applied Statistics from The Ohio State University (2016), an M.S. in Engineering Management from Missouri University of Science and Technology (2012), and a B.S. in Operations Research from The United States Military Academy (2007).
