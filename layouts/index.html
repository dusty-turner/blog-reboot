Dusty S Turner

Army Officer • Operations Research Systems Analyst • Statistician

I'm honored to have served 18 years in the Army, currently as a LTC. My career began as an Engineer Officer and now I am an Operations Research Systems Analyst. I am the author of the ggvfields package alongside David Kahle and Rodney Sturdivant. I’m also grateful for the support of my wife, Jill, and our two children—a 12-year-old son Cal and a 10-year-old daughter Reese.

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Statistical Science at Baylor University (anticipated 2025). I also hold an M.S. in Integrated Systems Engineering and a Graduate Minor in Applied Statistics from The Ohio State University (2016), an M.S. in Engineering Management from Missouri University of Science and Technology (2012), and a B.S. in Operations Research from The United States Military Academy (2007).
